Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. Bangladesh 2.0 refers to the vision of a modern, digital, and progressive Bangladesh. It emphasizes technological advancement, innovation, and economic growth, aiming to position the country as a leader in the global digital economy. With increased internet penetration, a thriving startup ecosystem, and government initiatives such as Digital Bangladesh, the nation is embracing the future. Bangladesh 2.0 focuses on harnessing technology to improve education, healthcare, and governance, ensuring sustainable development and prosperity for its citizens in the digital age.
